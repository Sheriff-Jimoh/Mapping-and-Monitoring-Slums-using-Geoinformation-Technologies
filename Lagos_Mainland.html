SLUM MAPPING AND MONITORING

MAPPING AND MONITORING SLUMS

USING GEOINFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES


This web application was developed for my masters thesis titled "Mapping and Monitoring Slums Using Geoinformation Technologies" during the Copernicus Master in Digital Earth Programme at the Paris Lodron University Salzburg, Austria and Palacky University Olomouc, Czech Republic. It showcased slum mapping and monitoring in Lagos Mainland Local Government Area of Lagos State, Nigeria.

The pixel-based, object-based and the deep learning methods were adopted for the slum mapping and the results of these methods with their respective algorithms are shown in the map interface with well-descriptive layers.

Overall, the object-based support vector machine algorithm performed better than the other algorithms.
